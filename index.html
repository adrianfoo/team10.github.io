Completing a level

  1. Starting the stage, player will be give a set of points.
  3. Get to the end point with points reminding to complete the level.
Scores

Every player starts off with 100 points in every scenario. Every time the player breaks a rule in the game, the points will be deducted.

  • Cycling on the wrong path: -1 point/second
  • Hits a vehicle: -10points
  • Hits a pedestrian: -10points
